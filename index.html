At Your Service

Swedish Massage

This style of massage releases muscular stress and relaxes the nervous system with gliding and kneading strokes on major muscle groups. The work can be targeted to a specific area of concern, or broadened to include the whole body.

Deep Tissue Work

Deeper work means relief from chronic muscular aches and pains. By releasing the superficial muscle tissues, targeted pressure can be safely applied to deeper layers of muscles, tendons, and fascia (the tissue covering muscles), releasing those painful knots and adhesions.

Prenatal Massage

Bodywork is crucial during times of transition, and pregnancy is no exception. This style of massage can aid with the common discomforts experienced by moms-to-be. Lymphatic drainage can help with reducing swelling, and targeted work can assist with strained back muscles.

Baby Massage

Massage is a wonderful way to enhance your baby’s growth and development, improve their (and your) sleep cycle, and provide an additional avenue for close and nurturing contact with your new baby. Ask about group classes and private tutorials!


Add On Services:

Cupping Theraphy

A Traditional Chinese Medicine Technique that uses cups to apply “reverse pressure” to targeted muscles areas. The “reverse pressure” increases circulation in these areas, loosening muscular adhesions, or “knots”, and allowing the therapist to more easily manipulate the area of concern. An excellent way to enhance Deep Tissue work.

Guided Breathwork

By incorporating guided breathwork into your massage, you can expect to activate your parasympathetic nervous system, the body’s switch to “Rest & Digest.” Find your way towards healing using your own breath and body by adding this to any of your services.

Dry Brushing

A practice with incredible health benefits, supporting lymphatic drainage and dead skin cell removal. It will leave your skin feeling invigorated and soft for days.
